This document explains the format of files in webice/data/beamline-properties directory.

- Energy flux
Used by raddose.

- polarization
Not used by webice.

- divergence
Not used by webice.

- energy_resolution
Not used by webice.

- beam_full
Not used by webice.

- beam_FWHM
Beam full width half maximum. Used by raddose.

- phi_speed
Used to give warning if calculated exposure time per degree is less than this speed.

- scanner
Not used by webice.

- overload_level
Not used by webice.

- gain
Not used by webice.

- adcoffset
Not used by webice.
